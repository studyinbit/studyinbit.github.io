## Section 2: The "Zero-Stress" Arrival System
### Headline
"We've Solved the Day 1 Logistics"

### Subheadline
"The things that usually stress parents out? We've got them covered."

### Three-Step Process (Visual timeline)

#### Step 1: Before You Fly
**"Pre-Arrival Preparation"**
- **Jastip (Jasa Titip) Service**:
  - Pre-order bedding sets
  - Cleaning supplies
  - Dorm essentials
  - Your room is ready when you arrive
- **WeChat Group Access**:
  - Money exchange services
  - Alipay setup guidance
  - Last-minute questions answered
- **Packing Guide**:
  - What to bring
  - What to buy in China
  - Luggage tips

#### Step 2: Arrival Day
**"From Airport to Dorm"**
- **Free Airport Shuttle**:
  - University-provided bus
  - Direct to Liangxiang campus
  - No navigating subway with 50kg luggage
- **BIND Welcome Team**:
  - Meet you at dorm
  - Help carry luggage
  - Show you around
- **Immediate Needs**:
  - First meal together
  - SIM card activation
  - Campus tour

#### Step 3: First Week
**"Getting Settled"**
- **Orientation Sessions**:
  - Campus facilities tour
  - Academic expectations
  - Cultural adjustment tips
- **Administrative Help**:
  - Bank account setup
  - Student ID activation
  - Health check completion
- **Social Integration**:
  - Meet your cohort
  - Join study groups
  - Attend welcome dinner

### Visual
- Illustrated timeline with photos at each step
- Checkmarks for completed items
- Warm, reassuring design

  - Secondary: Friendly blues and greens
  - Accents: Gold for highlights
- **Photography**: Candid, authentic photos of real students
- **Animations**:
  - Fade-in testimonials
