# Why BIT? - Content Plan

## Purpose
Establish credibility and differentiate BIT from other universities using hard data and strategic positioning. Address the "why not a local university?" question head-on.

## Hero Section
### Headline
"The Metrics That Actually Matter"

### Subheadline
"For Indonesian families, understanding the gap between general rankings and engineering power is critical. Here's the truth."

## Section 1: The "Seven Sons" Advantage
### Visual
- Animated infographic showing the 7 universities
- BIT highlighted with glow effect
- Connection lines to key industries: Aerospace, Defense, Manufacturing

**"Not Just a University—A National Priority"**

**What This Means for You:**
- **Priority Funding**: State-backed labs and research projects
- **Industry Connections**: Direct pipeline to China's most critical tech sectors
- **Double First-Class Status**: Class A designation—the gold standard

## Section 3: What Makes BIT Different?
### Three Pillars (Large Cards)

#### Pillar 1: "Engineering-First Culture"
- **Visual**: Image of labs, workshops, students building
- **Content**: "BIT is not for passive learning. It's for building. From day one, you're in labs, not just lecture halls."
- **Stats**:
  - 50+ research labs open to undergraduates
  - Average 3 major projects per year
  - *Note: Curriculum balance varies by major*

#### Pillar 2: "Industry Integration"
- **Visual**: Logos of partner companies (Huawei, BYD, etc.)
- **Content**: "Your professors aren't just academics—they're consultants for China's biggest tech companies."
- **Stats**:
  - 200+ industry partnerships
  - Strong recruitment from top Chinese tech firms
  - *Note: Opportunities may vary for international vs domestic students*

#### Pillar 3: "Global Mobility"
- **Visual**: World map with connection lines
- **Content**: "A BIT degree opens doors in China, Indonesia, and globally. You're not just studying—you're positioning yourself strategically."
- **Stats**:
  - Alumni in 50+ countries
  - Growing demand in Indonesian market (Huawei, BYD, TikTok)
  - Degree recognized internationally

## Design Notes
- **Layout**: Alternating left/right content blocks for visual rhythm
- **Typography**: Large, bold numbers for stats; clean body text
- **Colors**: Use data visualization colors (greens for positive, blues for neutral)
- **Animations**:
  - Table rows animate in sequence
  - Numbers count up when scrolled into view
  - Cards lift and glow on hover
- **Micro-interactions**: Tooltip explanations for technical terms
