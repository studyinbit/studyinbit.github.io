# Campus Life - Content Plan

## Purpose
Show, don't tell. Use rich visuals and specific details to help students and parents visualize daily life at BIT. Address the "where will my child live and eat?" concerns.

## Hero Section
### Headline
"Your Home in Beijing"

### Subheadline
"Modern facilities. Incredible food. A community that feels like family."

### Visual
- Full-width image carousel of campus life
- Overlay with subtle gradient
- Animated text reveal

## Design Notes
- **Layout**: Magazine-style with large images
- **Typography**: Clear hierarchy, readable body text
- **Colors**: Warm, inviting tones for food; cool, focused tones for study spaces
- **Animations**:
  - Image galleries with smooth transitions
  - Parallax scrolling for section breaks
  - Hover effects on cards with gradient blur from bottom
- **Photography**: Use actual campus photos from `/public/images/uni/`
- **Configurable**: Image hover effects and content easily editable
